FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What is check in/check out time?
  • Check in 3 p.m., check out 12 p.m.

  • Do you allow smoking on property? In the rooms?
  • We are a Non-Smoking hotel.

  • Do you have a fitness center? What kind of equipment does it have? What are the hours?
  • Yes, it is open 24 hours a day. It includes treadmills, free weights, elliptical machines and a stationary bike.

  • Will your rooms allow rollaways/cribs/playards? Is there a charge?
  • We do provide cribs complimentary; however we do not have rollaways.

  • What is your cancellation/deposit policy?
  • If booked directly through the Hotel, we have a 24 Hour prior to arrival cancellation policy on most reservations. You must cancel by 6 p.m. Eastern Standard time the day prior to arrival to avoid penalty. On certain reservations a special cancellation may apply. If booked through a third party, please contact the booking agent directly for cancellation policies.

  • What are the hours of the restaurant/bar/lounge?
  • Breakfast: Monday – Friday, 6:30 a.m. – 10:30 a.m. Saturday – Sunday, 7 a.m. – 11 a.m. Lunch: Wednesdays only, 12 noon – 2 p.m. Dinner: Sunday – Thursday, 5 p.m. – 10 p.m. Friday – Saturday, 5 p.m. – 11 p.m. Bar: Daily, 3 p.m. – 1 a.m.

  • Are pets allowed? Are there restrictions for weight, height or types?
  • Yes, The Muse is a pet-friendly hotel. All pets are welcome. Any type of pet is allowed with no weight or size restrictions. No charge or deposit, however there is a waiver required at check-in. No limit on the number of pets allowed

  • Do you offer special group rates?
  • Kimpton Hotels offers special rates during certain times of the year and the week. Please find a list of our Hot Dates, Great Rates and book your next meeting at a Kimpton Hotel.
