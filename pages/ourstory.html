OUR STORY

VISHNU SAGAR RESTUARANT:

It all began on February 17, 2005, when Mr. Sendhuil Kumar opened the Vishnu Sagar Hotel in Bangalore, Indiana. Sendhil’s goal was to own not just a family restaurant and not just a veg restaurant, but a place where everyone, of all ages, could come and have a great meal and great fun for a great price.

We like to brag about our Masal Dosa, Vanilla Pie, Made-From-Scratch Sides, and Fresh-Baked Bread. Everything we do goes into making our hearty meals stand out. We handcraft almost everything we serve. We provide larger portions so you get more food for your dollar. And if you want an Ice Cold Beer or Legendary Margarita to wash it all down — well, we have those, too.

At Vishnu Sagar, our team has an incredible sense of pride in everything we do. Our passion ensures you have a Legendary Experience every time. Because of our passion for our guests and our communities, our team members are involved outside the restaurants in their local communities as a way to give back. The Vishnu Sagar story is simple. Legendary Food, Legendary Service® — all with lots of Legendary Fun!
